About

New Zealand is famous for its dramatic landscape, and Auckland doesn’t disappoint. In addition to gorgeous views, Auckland has some of the best food available in the area, and at very reasonable prices. The relaxed Kiwi way of life is a draw for people all over the world.

Sheep Rule! There are 9 sheep per each person in New Zealand, making it the highest ratio in the world.

1. Waitomo Glowworm Caves

If you wish to experience something really beautiful and unique then this could be the idle place for you. The cave is naturally illuminated by thousands of glowworms. This is a site which you have never seen in your life before This is actually one of the most unique places to visit in New Zealand

2. Milford Sound,
South Island

A World Heritage Site, Fiordland National Park protects some of the most spectacular scenery in the country. the park is a haven for hikers with some of the country's best hiking, including the famous Milford Track Aquatic sports and fishing are popular recreational activities, as are hiking, mountaineering, and rock climbing.

3. Hobbiton Movie Set

On the Hobbiton Movie Set you’ll find the lush rolling hills of The Shire where you can wander past Hobbit Holes and have a drink in the Green Dragon Inn. The two-hour tour begins with a short bus ride from The Shire’s Rest to the farm. On the way they show videos with behind the scenes footage. 44 Hobbit Holes are spread over the hillside and there’s lots of green space. It really does feel like a village, albeit a rather unusual and picturesque one.
